Citation Guidelines

When citing the UCLA 3D Osteology Collection or specific models in your research, please use the following format:

For the Collection as a Whole:

UCLA 3D Osteology Collection. (Year). UCLA 3D Osteology Collection [Digital repository]. UCLA Department of Anthropology. https://ucla3dosteology.edu

For Individual Models:

UCLA 3D Osteology Collection. (Year). [Bone Name] [3D Model]. [Collection Type] Collection. Retrieved Month Day, Year, from https://ucla3dosteology.edu/collections/[collection-type]/[model-id]

Example:

UCLA 3D Osteology Collection. (2024). Cervical Vertebra C1 [3D Model]. Axial Collection. Retrieved March 28, 2024, from https://ucla3dosteology.edu/collections/axial/c1
